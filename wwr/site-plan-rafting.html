Overview

Purpose

The Rafting business's online presence aims to attract new customers, showcase services provide easy booking options, build a community, increase brand awareness, stay competitive, and offer customer support.

Audience

What is the age range of our target audience? - Our target audience is aged 18-50.

What type of activities is our target audience interested in? - Our target audience is interested in water rafting and related outdoor experiences, such as hiking, camping and kayaking.

What is the level of experience of our target audience? - Our target audience may be experienced rafters or beginners, and are likely to be physically active and willing to take calculated risks.
