Overview

Purpose

The Rafting business's online presence aims to attract new customers, showcase services provide easy booking options, build a community, increase brand awareness, stay competitive, and offer customer support.

Audience

The water rafting website targets adventure-seeking individuals and groups aged 18-50, interested in water rafting and related outdoor experiences. They may be experienced rafters or beginners, and are likely to be physically active, enjoy nature, and take calculated risks. The website caters to both groups, offering guided trips and instruction for those new to rafting.
