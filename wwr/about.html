Water White Rafting

Our water rafting trips offer thrilling rapids, stunning scenery, and challenges, suitable for all ages and skill levels, with expert guides leading through Class III-IV rapids.

History

Water rafting has a rich history dating back to ancient cultures, serving as a transportation, trade, and exploration mode. It gained popularity in the 19th century in the United States, and in the 1960s and 1970s, inflatable rafts and safety equipment made it accessible to all ages and skill levels.
